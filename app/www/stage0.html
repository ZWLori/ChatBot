Michelle

Virtual Assistant

Hello! I'm Michelle,

your conversational virtual assistant.

Michelle is a conversational personal finance assistant who can help you with basic tasks, for example setting up a bank account. Using the information that you provide, Michelle is able to compute the best recommendation just for you. Michelle is designed by the Customer Experience and Tech team at RBT bank, who have 8 combined years of customer experience and 9 combined years of computer programming experience.

Today, you will go through a demonstration session where Michelle will help you with three simple personal finance tasks:
1. Choosing a bank savings account
2. Choosing a home loan plan
3. Choosing a life insurance policy
